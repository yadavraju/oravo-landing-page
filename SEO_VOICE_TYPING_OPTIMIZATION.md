# SEO Optimization: "Voice Typing" Primary Keyword Strategy

## Executive Summary

Successfully refocused SEO strategy to prioritize **"voice typing"** as the primary keyword over "voice dictation" based on consumer search behavior analysis and search volume data.

**Key Insight:** "Voice typing" has significantly higher consumer search volume and is the preferred term for mainstream productivity tools (Google Docs Voice Typing, Chrome extensions, mobile keyboards), while "voice dictation" is more niche and professional-focused.
